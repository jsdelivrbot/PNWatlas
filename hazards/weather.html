Quick Facts:
⚬ Since 2000, there have been over 4500 recorded extreme weather events in the PNW. Of these, over 140 caused greater than $1 million worth of damage.

⚬ A hailstorm near Buena, WA in 2001 was the most damaging extreme weather event since 2000, causing over $70 million worth of damage, primarily to crops.

⚬ A debris flow near Oso, WA in 2014 had the highest death toll of any recorded extreme weather event since 2000, killing 43 people.

⚬ 2006 had the largest number of extreme weather events of any year since 2000, with over 480 recorded events.
