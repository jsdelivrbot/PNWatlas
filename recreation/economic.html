% of labor force employed in outdoor recreation industry


Quick Facts:
⚬ Of all U.S states, Idaho has the 10th highest percentage of its labor force working in jobs generated by the outdoor recreation industry. Oregon is 12th, Washington is 17th.


Quick Facts:
⚬ Of all PNW counties, Hood River County, OR has the largest percentage of its labor force working in the outdoor recreation industry.


Quick Facts:
⚬ Of all PNW counties, Multnomah County, OR has the largest percentage of its labor force working in local government parts and recreation organizations.


Quick Facts:
⚬ Of all U.S states, Idaho has the 10th highest per capita consumer spending on outdoor recreation. Oregon is 16th, Washington is 17th.


Quick Facts:
⚬ Of all PNW counties, King County, WA has the highest average annual wage for local government parks and recreation workers.


Quick Facts:
⚬ Of all U.S states, Washington has the 11th highest average annual wage for outdoor recreation workers. Oregon is 38th, Idaho is 39th.


Quick Facts:
⚬ Of all U.S states, Washington has the 10th highest outdoor recreation tax revenue per capita. Idaho is 14th, Oregon is 29th.
