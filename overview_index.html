Atlas of the Pacific Northwest

About the Atlas

The Atlas of the Pacific Northwest is a collection of maps, charts, and figures used to summarize the geographies of the Pacific Northwest. The atlas is divided into twelve chapters. Each chapter emphasizes a different geographic topic, and includes visualizations related to that topic.

The Atlas was created for the Valley Library at Oregon State University. The most recent edition of the atlas was published in 2003. This edition is the first that is fully online and interactive.
